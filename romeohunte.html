ROMEO HUNTE

As a Fashion Designer, Romeo Hunte’s inspiration started at the early age of 13.
With the leader of the inspiration being his mother. He has always had a keen eye for fashion by watching his mother’s sense of style.
Growing up, Hunte was additionally surrounded by multiple women that all had an appreciation for fashion.
These early influences helped Hunte to reach his destiny of becoming a fashion designer.
