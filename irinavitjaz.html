IRINA VITJAZ

Irina Vitjaz is a Haute Couture Russian-born Austrian designer whose pieces use delicate fabrics and matching hats to highlight the silhouette of the female body. The Russian fashion designer started out as a prodigy at a young age. Her inspirations came from cities all over the world, which led her to collect fabric, choosing a cosmopolitan style for her designs, and making her collections for her future. She tries to make her outfits both elegant and extravagance in order to make her own style.
